Han Solo

The Hero of All Anti-Heroes

A portrait of Han Solo wielding a blaster

"Han Solo is that character that we always wished we could be. I think most of us felt like Luke Skywalker, but we would have loved to have been Han Solo."

- Peter Jackson, director of the Lord of the Rings Trilogy

  • 29 BBY

    Born on the planet Corellia and orphaned at an early age.

  • 27 BBY

    Brought under the wings of Garris Shrike, bounty hunter turned criminal.

  • 12 BBY

    Won the Regional Sector Number Four's All-Human Free-For-All fight on Jubilar. First encounter with Boba Fett.

  • 10 BBY

    Narrowly escaped the clutches of Shrike, saved by Dewlanna, a Wookie who served as a cook on the ship. Accepted into the Academy of Carida and faced Shrike for a final confrontation. Another bounty hunter killed Shrike and Solo killed him.

  • 5 BBY

    Dispatched to aid Commander Nyklas in duties including slaving and construction work. Saved Chewbacca's life and escaped with the help of Rebel Alliance.

  • 4 BBY

    Befriended Mako Spince and began smuggling career using Nar Shaddaa as home base. Worked frequently for Jabba the Hutt. Also befriended Lando Calrissian.

  • 2.5 BBY

    Won against Lando in the Cloud City Sabaac Tournamente and gained Millenium Falcon as prize.

  • 0 BBY

    Did the infamous Kessel run in under 12 parsecs. In debt to Jabba. Met Obi-wan Kenobi and Luke Skywalker in a Mos Eisley cantina. Accepted job to transport the passengers to Alderaan which was blown up. The Falcon was drawn into the Death Star's tractor beam. Rescused Leia Organa and escaped. Became part of historic Battle of Yavin when Solo returned to help Luke blow up the Death Star.

  • 3 ABY

    An imperial drone discovered the rebel base in Hoth. The Battle of Hoth ensued. Solo managed to escape with Organa and C3PO to Cloud City but was betrayed by Calrissian. Solo and Organa fell in love but Solo was frozen in carbonite and shipped off to Jabba.

  • 4 ABY

    Solo rescued by his friends. Organa killed Jabba during the Sarlaac Pit battle. Participated in the Battle of Endor. Death Star destroyed with Emperor in it.

  • 8 ABY

    Married Leia Organa.

  • 34 ABY

    Killed by his son, Ben Solo, also known as Kylo Ren
